# Frontend Vision

## Mission

Provide managers and employees with the simplest possible experience for planning, tracking, and reviewing weekly commitments.

## UX Principles

### Simplicity First
Every core workflow should be completable in less than five minutes.

### Visibility Without Friction
Users should immediately understand commitments, risks, progress, and reliability.

### Action-Oriented Design
Users should always know what requires attention and what action is expected.

### Consistency
Forms, tables, filters, and detail views should follow common interaction patterns.

### Mobile-Friendly
Critical workflows must be accessible from mobile devices.

## Success Criteria

### Employee
Can complete Check-In and Check-Out within 5 minutes.

### Manager
Can understand team status within 5 minutes.
