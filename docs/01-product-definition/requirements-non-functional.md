Non-Functional Requirements

Introduction

This document defines the non-functional requirements for Priorities Tracker.

⸻

Security

NFR-001 Authentication

The platform shall require authentication for all users.

NFR-002 Authorization

The platform shall enforce role-based access control.

NFR-003 Data Protection

Sensitive information shall be protected in transit and at rest.

⸻

Performance

NFR-004 Response Time

Standard user operations should complete within acceptable interactive response times.

NFR-005 Scalability

The platform shall support growth in users, teams, projects, and historical data.

⸻

Availability

NFR-006 Availability

The platform should provide high availability for business users.

NFR-007 Backup and Recovery

The platform shall support data backup and recovery procedures.

⸻

Reliability

NFR-008 Data Integrity

The system shall preserve data consistency across planning cycles.

NFR-009 Auditability

Important actions shall be auditable.

⸻

Usability

NFR-010 Simplicity

Check-In and Check-Out processes should be completable within a few minutes.

NFR-011 Accessibility

The platform should be usable across common desktop and mobile browsers.

⸻

Maintainability

NFR-012 Observability

The platform shall provide logging and monitoring capabilities.

NFR-013 Extensibility

The architecture shall support future feature expansion.

⸻

Artificial Intelligence

NFR-014 Explainability

AI-generated insights should be understandable by users.

NFR-015 Transparency

AI outputs should be presented as recommendations rather than authoritative decisions.
