Product Positioning

Positioning Statement

Priorities Tracker is the platform that helps managers measure and improve execution reliability through simple, visible weekly commitments.

⸻

What We Are

Priorities Tracker is:

* A commitment management platform.
* An execution visibility platform.
* A reliability measurement platform.
* A management enablement tool.
* A Commitment Reliability Management solution.

⸻

What We Are Not

Priorities Tracker is not:

* A project management platform.
* A task management application.
* A sprint planning system.
* A time tracking tool.
* A resource planning solution.

⸻

Core Differentiator

Commitment Reliability Score (CRS)

Most tools answer:

What is someone working on?

Priorities Tracker answers:

How reliably does someone fulfill commitments?

⸻

Target Market

Primary market:

* Small teams
* Team managers
* Department leaders

Secondary market:

* Growing organizations
* Professional services teams
* Technology teams

⸻

Category Creation

Priorities Tracker establishes a new category:

Commitment Reliability Management

focused on visibility, accountability, and execution consistency.
