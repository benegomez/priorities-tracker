Success Metrics

Purpose

This document defines the key success metrics used to evaluate the adoption, effectiveness, and business value of Priorities Tracker.

The objective is to establish measurable indicators that demonstrate whether the platform is achieving its mission of improving execution visibility, commitment accountability, and delivery reliability.

⸻

Product Success Criteria

Priorities Tracker is considered successful when managers can consistently answer:

1. What did each employee commit to?
2. What did they complete?
3. How reliably do they fulfill commitments?

without relying on excessive meetings, manual reporting, or ad hoc status requests.

⸻

North Star Metric

Commitment Completion Rate

Definition

Percentage of committed priorities completed within the planned weekly cycle.

Formula

Completed Priorities
-------------------- × 100
Committed Priorities

Purpose

Measures the platform’s effectiveness in helping teams convert commitments into completed work.

⸻

Adoption Metrics

Weekly Active Users (WAU)

Definition

Number of unique users who actively use the platform during a planning cycle.

Objective

Measure platform adoption.

⸻

Check-In Completion Rate

Definition

Percentage of users who submit a weekly Check-In.

Formula

Completed Check-Ins
------------------- × 100
Eligible Users

Target

Greater than 90%.

⸻

Check-Out Completion Rate

Definition

Percentage of users who complete a weekly Check-Out.

Formula

Completed Check-Outs
-------------------- × 100
Eligible Users

Target

Greater than 85%.

⸻

Execution Metrics

Priority Completion Rate

Definition

Percentage of committed priorities completed during the same planning cycle.

Purpose

Measure execution effectiveness.

⸻

Task Completion Rate

Definition

Percentage of committed tasks completed.

Purpose

Provide additional execution visibility.

⸻

Carry-Over Rate

Definition

Percentage of priorities moved to future planning cycles.

Purpose

Identify planning and execution challenges.

⸻

Reliability Metrics

Average CRS

Definition

Average Commitment Reliability Score across a team or organization.

Purpose

Measure overall execution reliability.

⸻

CRS Trend

Definition

Change in average CRS over time.

Purpose

Identify improvement or deterioration trends.

⸻

High Reliability Population

Definition

Percentage of users with CRS greater than or equal to 90.

Purpose

Measure consistency of execution.

⸻

Manager Effectiveness Metrics

Team Visibility Time

Definition

Time required for a manager to understand team status.

Goal

Less than 5 minutes.

⸻

Meeting Preparation Time

Definition

Time required to prepare for one-on-one meetings.

Goal

Reduce preparation effort through dashboards and AI summaries.

⸻

AI Metrics

Weekly Summary Usage

Definition

Percentage of managers reviewing generated AI summaries.

Purpose

Measure AI adoption.

⸻

Insight Engagement

Definition

Percentage of AI-generated insights viewed by managers.

Purpose

Measure usefulness of AI capabilities.

⸻

Organizational Metrics

Risk Detection Rate

Definition

Percentage of risks identified before causing delivery impact.

Purpose

Measure proactive management effectiveness.

⸻

Blocker Resolution Time

Definition

Average time required to resolve reported blockers.

Purpose

Measure organizational responsiveness.

⸻

Long-Term Success Indicators

The long-term success of Priorities Tracker will be reflected by:

* Increased commitment completion rates.
* Higher CRS averages.
* Reduced carry-over rates.
* Reduced status meetings.
* Faster risk identification.
* Improved execution predictability.
* Increased management effectiveness.

⸻

Success Definition

Priorities Tracker succeeds when organizations gain a measurable improvement in execution visibility, commitment fulfillment, and reliability without increasing administrative overhead.
